Technology + Healthcare

AI Classification Research

Developed a machine learning classification approach for healthcare diagnosis prediction.

Project TypeGroup Project
My RoleData Analysis and Model Development
ImpactImproved classification evaluation and feature optimization process.
What I LearnedAI solutions depend on both technical quality and understanding stakeholder needs.

Challenge

Making prediction work meaningful for healthcare context.

The team needed to explore how classification models could support diagnosis prediction while balancing accuracy, feature relevance, and interpretability for people who depend on reliable decisions.

Action

Analyzed data, developed models, and refined evaluation.

I contributed to data preparation, model development, classification testing, and feature optimization so the team could compare performance and understand which variables influenced outcomes.

Result

A clearer evaluation process for model improvement.

The project improved how the team reviewed classification outcomes, identified stronger feature combinations, and discussed the practical value of machine learning in healthcare diagnosis support.

Reflection

Technical quality must serve human needs.

I learned that AI is not only about model scores. It requires empathy for users, awareness of decision consequences, and the discipline to communicate findings clearly.
