Business + Branding

Humility Apparel Brand Development

Supported the transformation of an online thrift business into an independent apparel brand.

Project TypeGroup Project
My RoleGraphic Designer, Product Photographer, Content Creator, Data Analyst Intern, HR Support, Quality Control, Admin Support
ImpactContributed to branding, content production, operations, and helped support sales of over 500 products within two months.
What I LearnedBuilding a successful brand requires collaboration between operations, branding, quality control, and customer understanding.

Challenge

Turning thrift operations into a more independent brand experience.

The business needed stronger visual identity, consistent product presentation, reliable operations, and customer-facing content that could support growth beyond reselling.

Action

Connected visual production with daily operations.

I supported design, photography, catalog creation, live-selling content, quality control, data analysis, administration, and team coordination to help the brand operate with more consistency.

Result

Brand, content, and operations moved together.

The team improved product presentation, maintained operational flow, and supported sales of more than 500 products within two months through coordinated creative and business execution.

Reflection

A brand is built by many small decisions.

I learned that customer trust is shaped by more than visuals. It comes from quality checks, team clarity, accurate data, consistent storytelling, and care in every operational detail.
